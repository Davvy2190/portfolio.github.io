What I Do

I am a Sound Designer and Game QA specialist with a passion for creating immersive player experiences through audio and meticulous testing. My work focuses on enhancing gameplay through high-quality sound design, clear audio feedback, and ensuring polished, bug-free releases. As a sound designer, I craft adaptive audio, sound effects, and interactive soundscapes that support gameplay mechanics and emotional engagement. As a QA tester, I apply structured testing methods to identify issues, improve usability, and help deliver stable and enjoyable player experiences. With experience in game engines, audio middleware, and collaborative development workflows, I bridge the gap between creative audio production and technical quality assurance..
